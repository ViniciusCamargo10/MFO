=== Configuração no cron-job.org ===

1. Crie conta em https://cron-job.org (gratuito)
2. Adicione um NOVO cron job (repita para cada horário):

   === Job 1 - 08:00 BRT ===
   Title: DOU Automation 08:00
   URL: https://api.github.com/repos/ViniciusCamargo10/MFO/actions/workflows/dou_automation.yml/dispatches
   Method: POST
   Custom Headers:
     Authorization: Bearer SEU_TOKEN_AQUI
     Content-Type: application/json
   Body: {"ref":"main"}
   Schedule: Every day at 11:00 (UTC) = 08:00 BRT

   === Job 2 - 16:00 BRT ===
   Title: DOU Automation 16:00
   URL: https://api.github.com/repos/ViniciusCamargo10/MFO/actions/workflows/dou_automation.yml/dispatches
   Method: POST
   Custom Headers:
     Authorization: Bearer SEU_TOKEN_AQUI
     Content-Type: application/json
   Body: {"ref":"main"}
   Schedule: Every day at 19:00 (UTC) = 16:00 BRT

=== Teste manual via PowerShell ===
