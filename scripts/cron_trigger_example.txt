=== Configuração no cron-job.org (ou similar) ===

1. Crie conta em https://cron-job.org
2. Adicione um novo cron job:

   Method: POST
   URL: https://api.github.com/repos/ViniciusCamargo10/MFO/actions/workflows/dou_automation.yml/dispatches
   Headers:
     Authorization: Bearer SEU_TOKEN_AQUI
     Content-Type: application/json
     Accept: application/vnd.github.v3+json
   Body: {"ref": "main"}
   Schedule: A cada dia às 08:00 BRT (11:00 UTC) e 16:00 BRT (19:00 UTC)

=== Teste manual via PowerShell ===
.\scripts\trigger_workflow.ps1 -Token "SEU_TOKEN"

=== Teste manual via curl ===
curl -X POST https://api.github.com/repos/ViniciusCamargo10/MFO/actions/workflows/dou_automation.yml/dispatches ^
  -H "Authorization: Bearer SEU_TOKEN" ^
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" ^
  -d "{\"ref\": \"main\"}"
